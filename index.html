About of UMS Laxminiya

The UMS Laxminiya, also known as chousa, is a premier institution for providing higher education in the state of Bihar. The high school is situated in Laxminiya Chousa, Madhepura of Bihar State in India Established on 01 July 1950, it is a public High school ideal for teaching and learning.
